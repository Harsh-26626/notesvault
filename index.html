NotesVault is a beginner-friendly open-source project hosted under the Open Source Society. Its purpose is to provide a collaborative platform for sharing academic notes, resources, and previous year questions (PYQs), making quality study material accessible to everyone.

  • Focus: Academic notes, resources, and PYQs
